# 🥗 NutriHub — Your Smart Nutrition Companion

NutriHub is a modern, user-friendly platform designed to help individuals make better dietary choices through personalized nutrition insights, tracking, and smart recommendations.

---

## 🚀 Features

- 🍽️ **Personalized Meal Plans**  
  Get customized meal suggestions based on your goals (weight loss, muscle gain, maintenance)

- 📊 **Nutrition Tracking**  
  Track calories, macros, and daily intake with ease

- 🧠 **AI-Powered Recommendations**  
  Smart suggestions based on your eating habits and preferences

- 🥦 **Food Database**  
  Access a wide range of foods with detailed nutritional information

- 📈 **Progress Monitoring**  
  Visualize your health journey with charts and insights

- 🔐 **User Authentication**  
  Secure login and personalized dashboard

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

**Frontend**

**Backend**
